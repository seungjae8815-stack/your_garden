# 나무 생성 프롬프트 (Gemini 이미지) — 5종 × 5단계

너의 정원 앱용 나무 일러스트를 **Gemini 2.5 Flash Image(별칭 Nano Banana)** 로
생성하는 프롬프트. 아래 5개는 **공통 규칙이 이미 포함된 완성형** — 코드블록을 그대로
복사해 붙여넣으면 된다. (꽃 프롬프트는 [flower_prompts.md] 참고)

## 앱 규격 메모
- 5단계 → `<species>_1.png` ~ `<species>_5.png` (cherry/maple/pine/ginkgo/persimmon)
- 나무는 땅에 심는 식물 — 앱이 흙(`soil.png`)을 합성하므로 그림엔 화분·흙더미·땅 없이
  **나무만**. 하단중앙에서 위로 자라게. 순백 배경으로 뽑고 후처리로 투명 처리.
- 화풍 통일: 첫 종 결과(또는 꽃 결과 1장)를 **레퍼런스로 첨부**하고 "same art style".

## 1. 벚나무 (Cherry blossom) 🌸

```
A single wide horizontal illustration showing the FIVE growth stages of ONE
flowering cherry tree (Prunus), arranged left to right in a row, in five
equal-width panels, evenly spaced.

Style: soft storybook watercolor illustration, gentle pastel colors, cozy
cottage-garden picture-book look, soft shading, no harsh outlines. Identical art
style, line weight, and color across all five stages.

The SAME tree growing — identical, botanically accurate leaves, bark, and crown
shape throughout, only the growth changes:
- Stage 1: a tiny seedling on a reddish short stem with two rounded seed-leaves
  (cotyledons) and one or two small oval finely-serrated leaves
- Stage 2: a slender sapling, a thin straight trunk with a few side twigs and
  small pointed oval serrated leaves; smooth bark
- Stage 3: a young tree with a fuller, rounded branching crown of green oval
  serrated leaves
- Stage 4: a maturing tree with a broad rounded spreading crown, smooth gray-brown
  bark marked with horizontal lenticel lines, and scattered pink flower buds
- Stage 5: a mature cherry tree in full bloom — a broad rounded canopy covered in
  clouds of soft pink five-petaled cherry blossoms, on a smooth gray-brown trunk

Requirements:
- All five trees grow straight up from the SAME ground baseline, rooted at the
  bottom, bottom-aligned; each later stage clearly taller and fuller than the previous
- NO flower pot, NO container, NO soil mound, NO ground, NO grass, NO cast shadow
  — just the tree itself
- Pure flat WHITE background (#FFFFFF), no props, no text, no labels, no numbers
- Each tree centered in its panel, full tree visible with a small top margin
- Front view at eye level; botanically accurate seedling, leaves, bark and form
```
